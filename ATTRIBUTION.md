## AI Assistance

Generative AI tools, primarily ChatGPT, were used to help with:

- narrowing the final project scope
- framing the project as a reproduction of the APEX workflow
- suggesting notebook organization and analysis workflow
- helping debug file paths, environment issues, and project framing against the course rubric

## What the Author Did

All AI-assisted content was reviewed, modified, and validated by the author. The author was responsible for:

- selecting the final reproduction target
- reading the official APEX repository and associated preprint
- cloning and inspecting the official codebase
- identifying the relevant scripts, configs, checkpoints, and outputs
- adapting the official HPC / SLURM workflow to a local notebook-based workflow where needed
- preparing local FASTA inputs for reproduction
- running the inference and explanation workflows
- documenting environment issues and deviations from the official setup
- designing and evaluating the extension or robustness analysis
- interpreting outputs and preparing final submission materials
